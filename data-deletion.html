Request data deletion

You can ask us to delete your Explaina account and the data we hold about you. We will process your request as described below.

What we will delete

  • Your account and profile (email, name, and other profile details)
  • Questions you have posted
  • Metadata for answers you have created (as a tutor)
  • Your YouTube connection (tokens and link to your channel) from our systems

What you should know

  • Videos on YouTube: If you published answer videos to YouTube via Explaina, those videos will stay on YouTube until you delete them yourself in your YouTube account. We only remove the link and tokens from our app.
  • Timeframe: We will confirm your request and aim to complete the deletion within a reasonable period (typically within 30 days).

How to request deletion

Send us an email from the email address associated with your Explaina account. We use it to verify your identity and find your data.
