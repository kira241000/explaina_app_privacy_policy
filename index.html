Privacy Policy for Explaina

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Explaina ("we", "our", or "the app") is a whiteboard-based video Q&A learning platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Explaina mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account and profile information

  • Email address (required for account creation and sign-in)
  • Full name (optional)
  • Gender (optional: male, female, other, prefer not to say)
  • Age (optional)
  • Role (student or tutor), to provide the right experience

If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email and, if you consent, your name and profile picture from Google.

1.2 Content you create

  • Questions: title, text content, subject, and tags you add when asking a question
  • Answers: when you record a whiteboard video answer as a tutor, we process and store metadata (e.g. title, description, duration, thumbnail) and the video is uploaded to YouTube if you have connected your channel
  • Recordings: video and audio are recorded on your device and may be uploaded to our backend and/or YouTube according to your choices

1.3 YouTube connection (tutors only)

If you connect your YouTube channel to publish answer videos:

  • We store a secure link between your Explaina account and your YouTube channel (channel ID, channel name)
  • We store encrypted access and refresh tokens to upload videos on your behalf
  • Video uploads, visibility (public/unlisted/private), and management on YouTube are subject to Google's Privacy Policy and YouTube's Terms of Service

1.4 Device and technical data

  • We may collect device type, operating system, and app version to support and improve the app
  • Local storage on your device is used for recordings and cache; we do not access other apps or files on your device beyond what the app needs to function

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and profile
  • Provide the learning platform: post questions, record and publish answers, and show content to students and tutors
  • Enable YouTube uploads when you connect your channel and choose to publish answers there
  • Respond to support requests and enforce our terms
  • Improve the app, fix bugs, and analyze usage in an aggregated way
  • Comply with applicable law and protect our rights and users' safety

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

  • Backend: Account, profile, questions, answers metadata, and YouTube connection data are stored using Supabase. Supabase processes data in accordance with its Privacy Policy and security practices.
  • Authentication: Sign-in is handled by Supabase Auth and, when you use Google, by Google Sign-In. We do not store your password when you use Google.
  • YouTube tokens: Access and refresh tokens for your YouTube channel are stored in encrypted form.
  • Recordings: Video/audio are recorded on your device; uploaded content is sent to our services and/or YouTube. We use industry-standard practices to protect data in transit and at rest.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and correction: You can view and update your profile and account details in the app.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us (see Section 8). Videos already published on YouTube may remain there unless you delete them in YouTube.
  • YouTube: You can disconnect your YouTube channel in the app at any time. Existing videos on YouTube remain under your control in your YouTube account.
  • Marketing: We do not use your data for third-party marketing.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or other regions with similar laws, you may have additional rights (e.g. access, portability, restriction, objection, withdrawal of consent, complaint to a supervisory authority). Contact us to exercise them.

6. Children's Privacy

Explaina is not directed at children under 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, through the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
